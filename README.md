## Stride
### You're further along than you think.
Stride captures what you learn every day — through voice or text — and turns it into structured proof of your growth. Resume bullets, STAR interview stories, skill trends. All built automatically from the moments you'd normally forget.

## What it does
Record or type what you worked on — Stride transcribes and structures it with AI
Organize by custom folder, whatever fits your life
Resume tab — weekly summaries, auto-generated bullets, and STAR stories ready for interviews
Tasks view — every log, searchable and sortable, grouped how you think
Profile calendar — see every day you showed up, visualized

## The problem it solves
Most people can't answer "tell me about a time you..." in an interview — not because they haven't done the work, but because they never wrote it down.
Stride fixes that. Speak for 30 seconds after your internship meeting. Type two lines after class. Stride does the rest.

## Tech Stack
